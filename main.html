The Great War of the Dream SMP

On the 31st of July, 2020, Wilbur Soot started an independent nation on the Dream SMP.
Our story starts with a smol dog, who had a big idea. He decided to join the big dogs on the Dream SMP, and fill the soulless oak slabs of the Prime Path with meaning.
His plan was simple. Starting a drug empire, whitch gives work and purpose to the Tommyinnitses of the server – it was something that every resonable man would’ve done. However, in the process of creating the perfect drug empire, when the golden carrots, fermented spider eyes and everything nice were added, they accidentally added an extra ingredient to the concoction – Revolution.
thus, l’manberg was born
on 2nd august 2020, dream, the server’s owner started a war against l’manberg, after wilbur soot declared “independance” from the dream smp.
the war started with the battle of the power tower, which - unfortunately - the dream team won. when the l’manbergians realized that their war-winning words can’t win the war, the 5th battalion fought epic battles after epic battles against the tyrants (except maybe when the right-hand man pulled a ‘charles lee‘ during the battle of the embassy) – and they were about to win.
but then, one of the l’manbergians, he who must not be named, committed betrayal; he mumbled something about how the revolution wasn’t meant to happen or some shit like that then activated a trap (all right, the right-hand man activated the trap - well done, mr. innit) which ended up killing all the revolutionaries.
after a failed attempt at negotiations and blowing up half of l’manberg, wilbur was ready to talk about surrender.
tommy still tried to challenge dream to a xiaolin showdown. he summoned all the courage he required, looked dream in the eye, aimed no higher – but maybe he was distracted by the fact that dream’s eyes were on his chest or somethin’, ‘cause he lost the duel.

at this stage, everything seemed to have been lost. until, tommy went into a one-on-one negotiation with dream, offering his precious discs in exchange for the independence of l’manberg
dream agreed to the terms and with that, tommy proved that the famous term ”words are our weapons” is true, and a war indeed can be won by them.
viva la revolución, viva l’manberg!
