The Great War of the Dream SMP

Thus, L’Manberg was born.

On August 2nd, 2020, Dream, the server’s owner started a war against L’Manberg, after Wilbur Soot declared „independance” from the Dream SMP.
The war started with the Battle of the Power Tower, which unfortunately the Dream Team won. After that (/then?), when the L’manbergians realized that their war-winning words can’t win the war, the 5th Battalion fought epic battles after epic battles against the tyrants (except maybe when the right hand man pulled a Charles Lee during the battle of the embassy) – and they were about to win.
